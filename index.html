How we work

A garage door company that tells you what it actually costs

A lot of what we see in Columbus is deferred maintenance — a spring that's been squeaking for a year, a cable that's fraying at the drum, an opener that works most of the time but skips every few cycles. About us

We know Columbus homes

Most of the homes in Columbus and the surrounding suburbs were built in the 1980s, 1990s, and 2000s. That means the original garage door hardware — the springs, cables, and openers — is aging out on a predictable schedule. Torsion springs on a door that goes up and down twice a day are rated for roughly 10,000 cycles. Do the math on a door that's been running for 15 years, and the spring is overdue.

We've serviced doors in just about every Columbus suburb — the newer construction in Lewis Center and Delaware County, the established neighborhoods in Upper Arlington and Bexley, the subdivisions in Hilliard and Reynoldsburg where half the neighborhood is calling us the same winter. We know what the common failure patterns are and we stock accordingly.

She had to leave for work at 6am and the garage was wide open. Spring had snapped during the night — you could see the break right at the winding cone. We were there within the hour and had her door operating normally before she left. That's the kind of call we take seriously."

Common repairs

The repairs we do most in Columbus

After years of running calls across Franklin County, the failure patterns are pretty consistent. Springs go in the winter. Cables snap when a spring fails and the load transfers wrong. Openers develop logic board issues around year twelve or thirteen. Rollers crack and fall out of track when they dry out.

The most common emergency call we get is a spring failure — typically a homeowner who goes out in the morning and the door won't open. The spring breaks at night, usually during a cold snap, and the opener can't lift the door without it. It's a loud pop when it goes; a lot of homeowners hear it from inside the house and don't know what it was until the next morning.

"On a Dublin call last spring, we found both torsion springs had failed — one had gone recently and one had gone some time before that. The homeowner had been using the door manually for a month, thinking the opener had failed. The opener was fine. Replacing both springs got the door operating normally in about 45 minutes."

Common questions

What people ask before they call

What areas do you serve in Columbus?

Columbus and all surrounding suburbs — Dublin, Westerville, Hilliard, Grove City, Gahanna, Reynoldsburg, Upper Arlington, and Lewis Center. If you're in Franklin County or just outside it, call us. We can usually get there same day.

Do you offer same-day garage door repair?

Yes. For most repairs — broken springs, cable failures, opener issues, doors off track — same-day scheduling is available. We take emergency calls nights and weekends as well, no extra charge.

How much does garage door repair cost in Columbus?

Spring replacement is typically $150 to $350. Cable repair runs $100 to $200. Opener repair or replacement is $150 to $400. New door installation starts around $800. You get a written quote before any work starts — no surprises on the invoice.

My spring broke. Is it safe to use the door?

No. Don't force a garage door open manually when a torsion spring has failed — they're under several hundred pounds of tension and the remaining hardware is under abnormal load. Leave the door in place and call us. Spring replacement is typically same day.

Can you repair my garage door opener, or do I need a new one?

Most opener problems are repairable. Circuit board failures, gear strip issues, and remote problems are all common repairs. If the opener is past fifteen years or the motor has failed, replacement is usually more cost-effective. We'll tell you honestly which situation you're in.

Do you do commercial garage door work?

Yes. We service commercial overhead doors and roll-up warehouse doors across the Columbus area. Commercial scheduling and pricing available — call us to discuss your property and timeline.

Need a garage door repaired today?

Same-day service across Columbus and Franklin County. We carry the parts most Columbus homes need — no waiting on orders. Call us or request a callback and we'll get back to you within the hour.
