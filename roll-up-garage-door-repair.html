Roll-up door repair for Columbus commercial properties

Roll-up doors — steel curtain doors that coil into a drum above the opening rather than traveling horizontal tracks — are common on Columbus-area warehouses, self-storage facilities, light industrial buildings, and commercial garages. They are a different mechanical system than residential sectional doors and require different tools and knowledge to service correctly.

We service roll-up doors across the Columbus area including the Rickenbacker corridor, the West Broad and Frank Road industrial areas, and self-storage facilities throughout Franklin County.

Common roll-up door repairs

Spring barrel assembly

Roll-up doors use a large coil spring inside the drum barrel rather than torsion springs on an exposed shaft. When the spring barrel fails, the door becomes extremely heavy and difficult to operate. Spring barrel replacement requires the door to be fully lowered, the drum de-tensioned, and the new spring set to correct tension for the door's weight and height. Not a repair to attempt without experience and proper equipment.

Curtain guide repair

The steel slat curtain runs in guides on each side of the opening. Bent, worn, or loose guides cause the curtain to bind or jump the guide. Bent guides are common after forklift impacts or loading dock collisions. Guide replacement or straightening restores smooth operation.

Chain hoist and motor operator service

Manual roll-up doors use a chain hoist to operate spring tension. Motorized doors use an electric operator. We service all operator types including high-cycle industrial operators.

Bottom bar and seal replacement

The bottom bar takes significant abuse from forklifts, pallet jacks, and vehicle contacts. A bent bottom bar prevents proper weather sealing and causes the curtain to bind. Bottom bar straightening or replacement is common maintenance on high-use commercial doors.

"Roll-up door spring failures in warehouse settings are priority calls — a door that will not close is both a security and weather issue. We carry coil spring sets for common roll-up door sizes and treat commercial calls with the same urgency as residential emergencies. A business that cannot close its loading dock overnight needs the same response as a homeowner with an open garage."
