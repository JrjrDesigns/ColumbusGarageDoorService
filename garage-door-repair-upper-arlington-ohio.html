Garage door service in Upper Arlington

We run regular calls throughout Upper Arlington and the surrounding area — Old Arlington, the Lane Avenue corridor, Tremont, Sunny 95 area, and neighborhoods along Tremont Road and Northwest Boulevard. Most of the homes here are in the range where original garage door hardware is starting to age out. Springs rated for 10,000 cycles wear down on a predictable schedule, and when one goes, we typically see a wave of calls from the same neighborhood over the following weeks.

We carry the springs, cables, and opener parts that fit the most common Upper Arlington-area residential doors. That means most repairs are completed same-visit — no waiting on a parts order.

We've worked on UA homes where the tracks are original to the house and the spring system has been retrofitted twice. Common questions from Upper Arlington homeowners

Do you service older Upper Arlington homes with non-standard garage openings?

Yes. Upper Arlington has a lot of mid-century homes with non-standard opening sizes. We measure before quoting and can accommodate most custom sizing situations.

My Upper Arlington garage was built in the 1950s — can you service the hardware?

Yes. We service older hardware including extension spring systems common on mid-century garages. If the hardware is beyond repair, we can quote a full system update to modern torsion spring hardware.

Do you charge extra for evenings or weekends?

No. Our pricing is the same regardless of when you call — evenings, weekends, and holiday calls are all billed at standard rates.

What's the typical arrival time for a Upper Arlington call?

For most Upper Arlington addresses, same-day service is available and we can typically arrive within a few hours of your call. We'll give you a specific window when you call.
